Forgotten Era is an extremely challenging exploration game of the Souls Like & Survival genre, set in a vast world devastated by the unknown. The player takes on the role of a survivor who must confront challenging monsters and complicated bosses, collecting valuable resources and using a wide variety of weapons and magic to progress.

The combat mechanics are precise and strategic, demanding skill and quick reflexes from the player. Each enemy has strengths and weaknesses, and players must learn to use them to their advantage in order to survive. Resource gathering is crucial for game progression as they enable the creation of weapons and magic, as well as character customization.
