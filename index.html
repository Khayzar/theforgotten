Look around, and maybe you will

"In the depths of an ancient world, where the shadows of past ages still whisper forbidden secrets, lie lands corrupted by eternal twilight. In these realms, where hope has long been forsaken, the few brave souls who dare to venture into the thick forests and treacherous valleys must be prepared to face horrors that defy sanity. No refuge is safe, for even the most serene fields conceal deadly traps, and beauty masks chaos. The paths are winding, filled with creatures that have roamed since the dawn of time, ever hungry for the unwary. To survive, fools must not only wield their swords or call upon ancient magics; they must master the art of the forge, the alchemy of craft, and the arduous toil of mining deep within the earth. Each weapon, every tool, and every potion is forged in the fire of necessity, for in this cruel world, the weak do not endure. Only the most valiant will withstand the trials of this ravaged realm. Those who do not fear the darkness, who understand that death lurks with every step, will forge their destiny in steel and blood. Among the ruins of forgotten civilizations and the whispers of forsaken gods, a new hero shall rise, but the path to glory will be paved with sacrifice and shadow."
